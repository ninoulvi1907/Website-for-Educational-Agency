About Us

Class Education is a leading educational agency in Azerbaijan, dedicated to helping students achieve their dreams of studying abroad. Founded in 2016, we have built a strong reputation for excellence in international education consulting.

We specialize in helping students from Azerbaijan, Turkey, Russia, Georgia, Iran, Kazakhstan, Mongolia, and Jordan gain admission to prestigious universities across Europe.

With a 98% acceptance rate and 90% of our students receiving scholarships, we are committed to making international education accessible and affordable.
