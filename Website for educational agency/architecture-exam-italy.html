Registration for Architecture Entrance Exams in Italy Has Started
Bachelor's Degree • Architecture • Italy

Applications are now open for Architecture programs at two of Italy's leading technical universities.

IMPORTANT 20 June, 2026

Architecture Applications Are Open in Italy

Applications have started for Bachelor's degree programs in Architecture at Politecnico di Milano (TOP 87) and Politecnico di Torino (TOP 206). Students can study at these universities with tuition waivers and scholarships.

Admission is carried out through an Architecture entrance exam. Registered students will receive sample questions and study materials to help them prepare for the exam.

Exam Details

  • Politecnico di Milano: the entrance exam will be held online on 23 July. The exam registration fee is 35 EUR.
  • Politecnico di Torino: the entrance exam will be held in person on 21 July. The exam registration fee is 50 EUR.
  • Sample questions and lesson materials are provided to registered students.
Application deadline: 5 July
